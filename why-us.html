FAQ

Find answers to the most common questions about DreamAlchemy

Concept

What exactly is DreamAlchemy? Is it a game or a meditation app?

It's a new category of wellness: Active Somatic Gaming. Unlike meditation apps where you passively listen, DreamAlchemy uses AI to turn your dream narratives into an interactive game. You use our physical companion, Lunara, to physically interact with the story—squeezing to crush fears or shaking to transform nightmares into positive outcomes. It's about doing, not just thinking.

How does the "Dream-to-Game" process work?

It takes less than a minute.

Log: When you wake up, briefly speak or type your dream into the app.

Generate: Our secure AI instantly analyzes the emotions and generates a personalized 3D visual level.

Play: You use the Lunara device to interact with the dream elements. Your physical actions (grip intensity, movement) determine how the dream is rewritten, helping you achieve emotional closure before you start your day.

Do I need to use it every day?

We recommend using it whenever you wake up with "emotional hangover"—feeling drained, anxious, or unsettled by a dream. Many users find that a quick 3-minute session in the morning sets a positive tone for the entire day.

Lunara Device

Can I use the app without the Lunara device?

While the app allows for basic dream logging, the core therapeutic experience requires Lunara. The science behind DreamAlchemy is based on Somatic (Body-Based) Processing. The haptic feedback and pressure sensors in Lunara allow your body to physically release tension. Without the hardware, you are missing the most critical part of the emotional release.

What does Lunara feel like?

Lunara is designed for comfort and grounding. It features a soft-touch, medical-grade silicone skin that feels organic, not like plastic electronics. It fits naturally in the palm of your hand and provides subtle vibration feedback that mimics a soothing heartbeat or a satisfying "crunch" when you defeat a nightmare monster.

How does Lunara connect to my phone?

It connects seamlessly via low-energy Bluetooth. Just wake up, squeeze Lunara to turn it on, and the app recognizes it instantly.

Science

Is this a replacement for therapy?

No. DreamAlchemy is a wellness tool for self-care, not a medical device or a substitute for professional clinical therapy. However, it is built on evidence-based psychological principles, including Imagery Rehearsal Therapy (IRT) and Gestalt Therapy techniques. If you suffer from PTSD or severe trauma, please consult a healthcare professional.

Why do you focus on "crushing" or changing the dream?

This is based on the concept of Agency Restoration. Nightmares often make us feel helpless. By actively rewriting the ending of a dream and physically manipulating the outcome using your hands, you signal to your brain that you are in control. This helps reduce the emotional intensity of the memory.

Privacy

Are my dreams private? Who can see them?

Your privacy is our absolute priority. Unlike many apps that upload data to the cloud, DreamAlchemy uses Edge AI technology. This means your dream text is processed locally on your device or within a secure encrypted enclave. We do not sell your personal dream data to advertisers, and no human at DreamAlchemy reads your dreams.

Do you record audio when I sleep?

Absolutely not. DreamAlchemy is an active intervention tool that you use after you wake up. We do not use microphones or cameras to monitor your sleep.
