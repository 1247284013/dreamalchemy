How It Works

Four Steps to Reclaim Your Dreams

STEP 1

Share Your Dream

Tell us what you dreamed — type it or speak it. A nightmare about drowning. A recurring dream about being chased. A strange scene you can't shake.

STEP 2

AI Builds Your World

Our AI transforms your dream content into a personalized interactive game environment. The monster you described? It's there. The dark room? You're in it. Every session is uniquely yours.

STEP 3

Take Physical Action

Using our companion toy, you interact with your dream world through real physical gestures — crush, soothe, shake, or blow. Your body leads the healing.

STEP 4

Feel the Shift

Watch the nightmare crumble under your grip. See the dark scene dissolve with your breath. Your physical actions directly reshape your dream world — turning fear into mastery, helplessness into agency.
