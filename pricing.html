For Organisations

Enterprise B2B Pricing

To tap into the professional market, we offer an Enterprise Tier starting at £15/month, utilising a per-employee billing model. We provide organisations with a fully anonymous, private environment for their members. This tier includes add-on services, such as direct pathways to professional counselling for employees or patients.

Hardware Bundle Policy: Our hardware bundle revenue policy for B2B clients mirrors our B2C strategy. First-time subscribers who purchase the hardware bundle for £69.99 will receive their first two months of the Value Based Tier for free.
